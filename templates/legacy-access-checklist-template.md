## Introduction

This checklist enumerates every artefact, credential, export, and contact we need from you to execute this engagement safely. It has been written assuming that the vendor or agency who originally built and maintained your legacy website is **no longer available to help** — every item includes guidance on where YOU can find it inside your own accounts, and a fallback for what we will do if it cannot be located.

**How to use this document:**

1. Walk through each section with the relevant person on your side (Marketing, IT, Finance, or Operations — the Customer Owner column tells you who).
2. In the **Provisioned?** column (add one as you return this to us), mark items as ✅ Provisioned, ⏳ In progress, or ❌ Cannot locate.
3. For any ❌ item, read the **Notes** column — it explains what we will do in place of the missing item. No item is a dead-end; we have a fallback for each.
4. Target to complete all **P0** items before our kickoff date. P1 items are due inside the first two sprints. P2 items can come later.

Priority legend:

- **P0** — blocking kickoff; needed before we can start.
- **P1** — blocking a mid-project milestone; required within the first two sprints.
- **P2** — enables optimisation / de-risks later phases; desirable but not blocking.
